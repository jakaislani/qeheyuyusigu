About

Fashion designer, actress, and TikTok star who rose to fame on her self-titled account, where she posts get ready with me videos and clips of her outfits often using everyday household items and office supplies. Her unconventional style has gained her 650,000 followers and over 20 million likes on the platform.

She made her TikTok debut in March 2020, uploading a video of styling tips for sheer tops.

Many of her "fit check" videos are done in what her fans call her keyboard bathroom, because there are keyboards affixed to the walls and ceiling. She also started her own clothing line, Magdalen Clothing, which she promotes to her 70,000 followers on her myramagdalen Instagram.

She was born and raised in Huntsville, Alabama. She has a cat.

In October 2022, she posted a TikTok set to a sped-up version of Thundercat's "Them Changes."
