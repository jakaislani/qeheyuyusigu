Sarah Michelle Gellar's Son Rocky Already Acting Like a Teenager: Pics

Helping out his famous mommy! Sarah Michelle Gellar’s son Rocky, 2, is growing up — and fast! The Buffy the Vampire Slayer alum shared new (and very rare!) photos of her little one this past week.

“This is what happens when everyone in your house is sick- You have to send your 2-year-old to the drugstore #MakeItWork,” Gellar, 37, captioned via Instagram. In the snapshot, Rocky — not revealing his face — grabbed some medicine from the shelf, possibly for his dad Freddie Prinze Jr., 38, and big sister Charlotte, 5.
