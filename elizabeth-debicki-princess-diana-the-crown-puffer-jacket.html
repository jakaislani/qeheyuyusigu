Elizabeth Debicki Replicated This '90s Princess Diana Outfit

The line between Elizabeth Debicki and Princess Diana continues to visually blur as season 5 of the Netflix royal drama The Crown films.

The latest double-take inducing image of Debicki features the actress in a red puffer jacket, black pants, a matching turtleneck, and slouchy brown boots, a replica of Diana's beloved navy Royal Canadian Mounted Police baseball cap atop her head.

The late royal sported a near-identical outfit during a 1994 visit to Austria with sons Prince William and Prince Harry.

Though we've essentially been waiting for season 5 since season 4 launched in late 2020, the Crown's release date is nearing. In September, Imelda Staunton (the series' third iteration of Queen Elizabeth) announced that the penultimate season will be premiering this November.

In addition to Debicki and Staunton, other season 5 cast additions include Lesley Manville (Princess Margaret), Jonathan Pryce (Prince Philip), and Dominic West (Prince Charles).
