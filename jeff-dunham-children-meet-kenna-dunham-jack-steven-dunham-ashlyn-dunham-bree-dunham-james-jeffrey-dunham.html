Jeff Dunham’s Children

Jeff Dunham has five children: Kenna Dunham, Jack Steven Dunham, Ashlyn Dunham, Bree Dunham and James Jeffrey Dunham.

Bree Dunham

Bree Dunham is the adopted daughter of Jeff Dunham and his first wife, Paige Dunham.  Bree was born on August 1991. Bree is 31 years old and the eldest daughter of Jeff Dunham.

Ashlyn Dunham

Ashlyn Dunham is the first biological daughter of Jeff Dunham and Paige Dunham. Ashlyn Dunham was born on September 11, 1995. Ashlyn is 27 years old.

Kenna Dunham

Kenna Dunham is the second biological daughter of Jeff and Paige. Kenna Dunham was born 25 years ago.

Jack and James Dunham

Jack and James are twins and the last borns of Jeff Dunham and his second wife, Audrey Murdrick. Jack and James were born on October 11 2015.
