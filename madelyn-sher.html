Vision Of The Artist

Madelyn is an artist influenced by dance theatre. She pursues the contemporary style of dance art and lets her body flow in a melodramatic way. Through her website, she has revealed that she is interested in textured physicality and the interconnectedness of the body and the earth.

According to her website, she has the following to say about her art:

The emerging focal points of my artistic practice include romanticism, shapeshifting, and melodrama. My dance creations are movement collages that reimagine the unfolding of time and the expression of the self. I am curious about the position of the contemporary body in light of environmental catastrophe that is needed to heal ourselves and our planet.

-madelynsher.com

Career As A Dancer

As a dancer, choreographer, performing artist, and producer, Madelyn has danced with Vangeline Theater. The artist is also a member of the Bronx Arts Ensemble and Zoo City Art Collective. 

She is a versatile performer who has performed in works presented by New Worlds Theater Project, Hippocampe Mime Corporel Théâtre. Beverly Lopez, Ni'Ja Whitson,  and the Harbor Lights Theater Company.

The choreographies of Madelyn have been presented at various famed theatres like SMUSH Gallery, New City, MERDE at The Footlight Downtown Art, and many more. And moreover, she made a short dance film named Looking Inwood, which won her the Promising Filmmaker Award at CUNY Film Festival 2018.

Let's Know About Madelyn's Educational Qualifications: 

According to sources, Madelyn went to Children's Harbor Montessori School as her elementary school. Then for middle school, she attended Professional Performing Arts School (PPAS) in Manhattan.

Later, Madelyn entered Laguardia Arts High School for her high school studies. And after graduating from high school, she went to CUNY Lehman College. And thus she is a graduate of B.A. in dance and philosophy from the Macaulay Honors Program. The source also states that Madelyn studied dance at The Ailey School of Dance.

And as per Madelyn's announcement on her website, she would enroll in the Fall of 2022, she is now pursuing a Master of Fine Arts in Choreography and Performance from Smith College.
