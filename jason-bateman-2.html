Career

Jason Bateman started his career in 1980 by appearing in a commercial for breakfast cereal called Golden Grahams. After that he entered the television Industry by appearing in a television series called ‘Little House on the Prairie’. From 1982-1984 he appeared in a television serial called ‘Silver spoons’.

He played a supporting character in the serial. In the same year he appeared in an episode of the television serial called Knight rider. From 1984-1985 he appeared in a television serial called It’s your move. In 1987 he appeared on a television game show called Win, Lose or draw.

He is best known for playing the role of David Hogan in The Hogan Family. In 1987 he was cast in an American fantasy movie called Teen Wolf Too which was a failure at the box office. In 1994 he was cast in a movie called This can’t be love.

Around the same time he was also a part of four television series but none of them failed to make a mark. He made his directories debut in 1999 by directing an episode of Two of it’s Kind. In 2004 he was part of a movie called The Sweetest thing. In 2003 he was selected to be a part of comedy series called Arrested development.
