Slogoman is an English social media star best known for his YouTube channel of the same name, which has amassed a fanbase of more than 7 million subscribers. On it, he has primarily played video games such as Minecraft, Grand Theft Auto V, and Fortnite: Battle Royale. Slogoman has also collaborated with fellow YouTubers Jelly, Kwebbelkop, and TheGamingLemon. He was also the 12th and last YouTuber to join a YouTuber group Revelmode in 2016, which was founded by PewDiePie and shut down on February 14, 2017.

    Slogoman Facts

  • He calls his fanbase members Slugs, Slogonators, or Slogoarmy.
  • He grew up in Farnham, Surrey, England, United Kingdom.
  • Slogoman used to hate slugs as a kid and in a weird twist, made his channel logo a slug and picked a channel name that resembled it.
  • His 1st YouTube channel was titled Oxymorose and had nothing to do with video games.
  • He met YouTubers Jelly and Kwebbelkop at a Germany Gamescom event in 2014. They later founded a YouTuber group Robust and were supposed to move in together into a house they named Robust Mansion. Slogoman was unable to do so due to his college obligations but visited on weekends.
  • Slogoman knew his best friend, YouTuber Brad “TheGamingLemon” Leman from a very young age. They attended the same school and lived just streets apart.
  • Slogoman was very interested in rugby during school and played a plethora of other sports. However, he had to vastly reduce the time dedicated to those activities after 2016, when his YouTube career took off.
  • He moved into his own house in late 2016.
  • He met DJ Marshmello in September 2018.
  • Slogoman’s YouTube channel reached a milestone of 1 million subscribers on November 19, 2015, and of 5 million subscribers on March 29, 2019.
  • His parents have 3 dogs, Lilly, Lotty, and Poppy. He has a dog named Rudy who has an official Instagram account “rudy.xyz” dedicated to him.
