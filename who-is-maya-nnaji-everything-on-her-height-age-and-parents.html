Maya Nnaji : Zeke Nnaji Sister

Maya Nnaji is the sister of NBA player Zeke Nnaji.

Zeke was drafted 22nd overall in the 2020 NBA Draft by the Denver Nuggets.

He also played for Hopkins High School. He had an average score of 24.1 points and 9.4 rebounds during his senior high school.

He played for the Arizona Wildcats but was declared for the 2020 NBA draft after his first year in college. The Denver Nuggets signed him on the 1st of December, 2020.

Maya Nnaji is 19 years old as of May 2021.

They love traveling as they have been to the United Kingdom, Germany, France, Belgium, and Nigeria.
