Christian Gap Year Programs | GoAbroad.com

Returned gappers often say they gained more from Christian gap year programs than they gave. That’s because whether you’re doing Christian gap semester programs, Christian gap year programs in the U.S., or year long mission trips for college students, you’ll grow exponentially! So, how can you get all this enlightenment?

Start your gap year by asking yourself what you can learn. You’ll be facing different ways of life abroad, but instead of seeing them as inferior, treat them respectfully and with an open mind. Take time to get to know locals without forcing your beliefs onto them. You’ll be more effective at reaching your mission goals and return home with a new appreciation for how different lifestyles are often just as effective.

Before you arrive, you may peep an in-country need that you can help fill. Maybe there’s a girl’s school lacking enough sanitary napkins for students. You could fundraise at church to buy feminine supplies for the school. So, chat with your Christian gap year program advisors for the lowdown on extra ways to help out. There could be a needy cause waiting for you to take up!

Alright, ready to embark on your greatest mission yet?! That’s right. Christian gap year programs are that major. Christian gap years are life changing because they mold selfless individuals and make for dope but impactful cross cultural exchange. And while you won’t be walking on water, you’ll be performing teenie miracles and leaving a bright mark on the world and on yourself. From gap year opportunities for high school graduates to year long mission trips for college students, gap year missions are all the rave. So, get ready to eat, pray, and play together in the spirit of fellowship. Godspeed.

Keep reading! Your future self will thank you for these benefits of a gap year program!
