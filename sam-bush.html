Biography

Sam Bush was born in Bowling Green, KY on April 13, 1952. Mandolin player widely credited with originating the bluegrass subgenre known as Newgrass. He was named Mandolin Player of the Year by the International Bluegrass Music Association in 1990, 1991, 1992 and 2007. He shared his Album of the Year Grammy with artists like fiddle player Alison Krauss. He won the National Fiddle Championship at age fifteen.
