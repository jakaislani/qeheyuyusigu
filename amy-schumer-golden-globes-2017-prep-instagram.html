Amy Schumer Is Prepping for the Golden Globes in the Best Way Possible

Amy Schumer isn't about that awards show detox life.

The comedienne, who is on her way to present at the 74th annual Golden Globe Awards—apparently, by way of a moving sidewalk if a recent Instagram video is any indication—and she has a super relaxed mentality about the whole event.

While many of the stars who will be in attendance have been getting ready with strict eating plans and fitness routines, it appears as if Schumer is just treating it like any old regular day, and we love her even more for it. The 35-year-old took to social media last night to reveal how she's gearing up, and shared a photo that features her sitting at a table covered with cups of coffee, beer, and tea while wearing a pair of oversize sunglasses and sparkly fairy wings.

"Golden Globe prep" she simply captioned the 'gram.

VIDEO: 13 of Amy Schumer's Funniest Instagram Photos

But this isn't her first trip to the Hollywood soirée. Last year, Schumer was nominated for a Golden Globe for Best Actress in a Motion Picture Comedy or Musical for her role in Trainwreck, and she wowed on the red carpet in a black-and-white gown by Prabal Gurung.

Tune in to the Jimmy Fallon-hosted Golden Globe ceremony this Sunday, Jan. 8, at 8 p.m. ET on NBC.
