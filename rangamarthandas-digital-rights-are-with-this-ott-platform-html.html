Rangamarthanda’s digital rights are with this OTT platform

Published on Mar 16, 2023 1:00 pm IST

Creative director Krishna Vamsi’s upcoming emotional drama Rangamarthanda is all set for a grand release in theaters on March 22, 2023. Prakash Raj, Ramya Krishnan and Brahmanandam are the lead cast in this flick.

The latest news is that the movie’s digital streaming rights are with the leading OTT platform, Amazon Prime Video. As per the sources, the OTT platform acquired the rights for a decent price.

Produced by Housefull Movies and Rajashyamala Entertainments, the film has Shivathmika Rajashekar, Rahul Sipligunj, Adarsh Balakrishna and Anasuya Bharadwaj in crucial roles. Isagnani Ilaiyaraaja composed the music for this star-studded film.
